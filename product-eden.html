Eden research board

Research software—not a mode inside finance

Eden is its own product: a local-first system for aggregating, analyzing, prioritizing, and evaluating sources. It is not a feature tucked inside CFO or Ledger.

Material is organized into claims, facts, and context. Summaries remain tied to sources, with structured outputs you can audit.

What Eden ingests

  • Screen recordings
  • Video—local files or public platforms such as YouTube, Rumble, Vimeo, or Instagram
  • Articles, documents, images, and screenshots
  • Your own files alongside sources you choose

Eden transcribes audio, runs OCR where needed, and produces structured research with citations.

Steward prioritizes what matters to you

Steward is logic-driven, not open-ended generation. In Eden it helps prioritize facts using your context—so the same material can be ordered differently for different questions.

Sources are weighed for consistency, evidence, cross-source agreement, and relevance. Eden clarifies the landscape; it does not replace your judgment.

Source confidence rules-based

What Eden is not

  • —It does not write papers or replace authorship.
  • —It does not pass judgment on people.
  • —It does not bypass paywalls or harvest restricted content.

Related products, separate missions

CFO is the financial operating system. Ledger is the identity vault and document layer. Eden answers a different question: turning diverse sources into prioritized, citable research—on your machine.
