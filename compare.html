CFO and the “fintech default”

Many apps bundle bank feeds, credit scores, and cross-sell into one noisy surface—optimized for time-in-app, not clarity. CFO is intentionally different: a local-first financial operating system with deterministic Steward logic, structured inputs, and boundaries—so finance stays finance, not a feed.

Eden and Ledger answer different questions entirely (research and identity/documents). None of our titles are designed to blur those missions for engagement metrics.
