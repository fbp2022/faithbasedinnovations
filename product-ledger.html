Vault first—then documents, filled exactly

Ledger is a local-first encrypted identity vault and deterministic document prefilling system—built for sensitive data and precise execution, not as a secondary tab inside another app.

Store identity fields (including sensitive items such as a Social Security number if you choose), address and employment history, relationships, documents, and profiles for up to two people—for example, spouses—all encrypted on your machine.

People and references

Track people you have known: relationship type, organization, role, relevance, and when you met.

Duration is calculated from local system time, so “how long you have known someone” stays accurate without manual edits.

Deterministic filling—never invented answers

Ledger maps document fields to stored values and fills only exact matches. Missing data stays empty—never guessed or inferred.

Steward helps parse documents, map fields, and guide workflows. It does not generate answers you did not provide.

Alongside CFO and Eden

CFO handles financial operating logic. Eden handles research ingestion and evaluation. Ledger handles identity, references, and document execution—three products, one standard, connected through Steward.
