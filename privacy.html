Privacy

Privacy policy

Pre-release context

We are in pre-release. This policy describes how we intend to handle data as products and commerce go live; we will publish dated updates when practices change.

Our default posture is simple: collect less, keep data local when possible, and avoid surveillance-style product behavior.

Overview

Faith Based Innovations, LLC designs products around local-first operation. That architecture naturally supports stronger data control and lower collection volume.

What we collect

We collect only what is necessary to support product delivery, licensing, and commerce workflows. Scope is minimized by design.

What we do not collect

We do not build broad behavior-tracking systems for ad targeting. We do not treat user activity as a growth-hacking dataset.

Where data lives

Primary application data is intended to remain on your device unless a specific feature explicitly requires external services.

Payments and third parties

Payments will be handled by specialized processors. Final provider details are published when integrations are production-ready.

Data deletion

Local data can be deleted directly by users. Account-level deletion support is provided according to product and licensing model.

Policy updates

Material updates to this policy will include effective dates and clear revision language.
