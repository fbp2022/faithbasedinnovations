CFO dashboard

A financial operating system—not a dashboard toy

CFO is built for money in motion: structure, planning, and decisions grounded in your data. It is not a generic tracker or a thin budgeting app—it interprets, analyzes, and guides using rules and real inputs.

Steward inside CFO interprets financial data, surfaces patterns and risks, and calculates projections and runway. Guidance is deterministic: tied to what you record, not invented.

What CFO handles

  • Budgeting and financial planning
  • Forecasting and cash-flow clarity
  • OCR ingestion for statements and documents
  • Optional Plaid integration—explicit and optional, never a silent default
  • Structured financial intelligence and advanced organization

Local-first and privacy-first. Your financial data stays oriented to your machine and your choices.

Steward without guesswork

Steward is a controlled, rules-based layer—not vague automation. In CFO, projections and signals trace back to sources and policy.

No fabricated balances or filler numbers. Where information is missing, CFO does not pretend otherwise.
