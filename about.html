About

A software company built around ownership

Faith Based Innovations, LLC builds premium desktop software for people who value control, stable tooling, and clear boundaries around their data.

Why local-first matters

Local-first software reduces dependency risk, keeps primary workflows available, and limits how much operational trust is outsourced.

When the core experience runs on your device, reliability and autonomy both improve.

Why ownership matters

Your records and workflows should not depend on an external growth strategy. Ownership-first design keeps control with the operator, not the platform.

That includes data access, continuity, and practical portability.

Why premium desktop software still matters

Not every serious workflow belongs in a browser tab. Desktop software still provides depth, speed, and predictable behavior for work that has consequences.

What we build

CFO, Ledger, and Eden are built with deliberate scope and durable foundations. The goal is software that remains useful after the novelty cycle ends.

Calm products. Serious outcomes.
